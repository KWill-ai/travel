What To Do on Layovers (CDG)

Follow this quick plan to reduce stress, avoid missed connections, and keep your documents ready.

1
Check your next gate and terminal immediately
Use airport screens or the airline app. Confirm gate changes every 30–45 minutes.
2
Confirm if you need passport control / security transfer
International connections may require immigration and re-screening depending on terminals.
3
Plan your buffer time
Aim to be near the next gate at least 60–90 minutes before boarding.
4
Refill water + light meal
Hydration helps. Avoid heavy meals if you’re prone to jet lag or nausea.
5
Charge devices + download offline docs
Boarding passes, passport scans, hotel details, contact list—save offline.
6
Rest smart
If you nap, set an alarm and keep valuables secured. Consider a lounge day-pass if needed.
